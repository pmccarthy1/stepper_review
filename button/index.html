Notes & Open Questions

Font: Figma specifies “Forever Forma Body Reg” at 16px weight 400 (Regular — not Medium). This prototype falls back to Inter while the webfont is unavailable.


Icon: The prototype uses inline SVG placeholders (24px, matching Figma spec). Icons use currentColor and automatically adapt: white on dark Fill, dark on Outline, dark on Focus surface, and gray on Inactive.


Hover: Fill and Outline hover states do NOT change the background color. The only visual change is a 2px underline appearing on the label — white for Fill, dark for Outline. Confirmed via Figma anatomy screenshot.


Pressed: rgba(40, 24, 5, 0.6) — Warm Black 850 at 60% alpha. Applied to the Fill background and to the Outline border+label. Not a darker solid hex.


Focus: A full visual transformation: Fill switches to #E0ECFE (Focus surface) with dark label text, a 2px white inner border, and a 2px Core Blue (#0061F4) outer ring. Applies to both Fill and Outline variants identically.


Inactive: Solid #ABABAB (Neutral 350) — a distinct flat gray, not the resting color at reduced opacity.
